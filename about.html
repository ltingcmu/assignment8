About me


I'm Lily, a product designer with a background in visual arts, business analytics, and entrepreneurship. I love working at the intersection of my analytic and creative skillsets.


I was most recently an associate at Goldman Sachs in New York City. In equity trading, I managed relationships with prominent corporate clients, executed unique financial transactions, and optimized inefficient processes. Building data analytics dashboards sparked my interest in product design, and led me to pursue my Master of Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University.

I graduated from the University of Chicago in 2017 with a B.A. in Public Policy. I was also a collegiate athlete, competing in varsity cross country & track. I'm originally from Los Angeles.
