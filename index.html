Kellogg Design Challenge

Unilever sponsored a case competition aiming to innovate sustainability & Vaseline.

Problem Vaseline needed a new, innovative, and more sustainable consumer experience.

Solution An interactive system that encourages brand loyalty and sustainable behaviors through education and gamifying recycling.

Methods Semi-structured interviews, intercept interviews, participatory design, user archetypes


ReportChat

A chat-bot that appears after a user reports an ad, aiming to enhance the reporting process and target algorithmic bias.

Problem Users aren't motivated to report ads because of the lack of feedback and feedforward.

Solution A follow-up chatbot that provides stronger feedback, and encourages users to continue to audit algorithmic bias.

Methods Semi-structured interviews, contextual interviews, speed dating
